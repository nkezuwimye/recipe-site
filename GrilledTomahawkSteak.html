GrilledTomahawkSteak

ingradients

  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Montreal steak seasonin
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1 (2 3/4 pound) tomahawk steak
  • (3-inch thick bone-in ribeye)

steps

  1. ITo make steak rub, place brown sugar, Montreal steak seasoning, garlic powder, and onion powder in a small airtight container; shake or stir to combine. Set aside 2 teaspoons steak rub to use in compound butter.
  2. Place steak on a half sheet pan, and rub steak on top, bottom, and sides with remaining steak rub until well coated. Place steak, uncovered, in the refrigerator to dry-brine overnight.
